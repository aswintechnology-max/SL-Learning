📘 Study Tips for Students

⏰ Time Management

Plan your day. Study for short intervals and take small breaks.

📝 Practice

Revise daily. Solve sample papers to improve confidence.

💤 Rest

Good sleep keeps your brain active and fresh for learning.

🤝 Ask for Help

Never hesitate to ask teachers or friends when in doubt.

🎶 Hobbies & Talents Matter

🎤 Music

Singing or playing instruments can reduce stress and build confidence.

🎨 Drawing & Painting

🏃 Sports

Sports not only keep you healthy but also teach teamwork & discipline.

📖 Reading

Books improve imagination, vocabulary, and knowledge.
